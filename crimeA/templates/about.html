Is my report confidential?

Yes, all reports are handled with utmost confidentiality to ensure your safety and privacy.

How do I track the status of my report?

You can track your report through your dashboard, where updates on actions taken will be provided.

Can I report anonymously?

Yes, you can report anonymously. Simply select the anonymous option when submitting your report.

How long does it take to process a report?

The processing time varies depending on the complexity of the case, but we aim to respond within 24-48 hours.
